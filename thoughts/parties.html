My thoughts on missing out

The feeling of missing out on something you know you wouldn't have liked in the first place is a wierd feeling, I wish there was a word for this, since fomo doesn't quite capture it. Fomo implies the reason I am afraid of missing out, is because I want to have fun, but what I feel is that, by entering the activity I would not have fun. What I am feeling is jealosy. Why can someone derive joy from dancing, jumping and singing and I can not. Why does the same shrill of joy screeching from the audience eminate into my eardrums as stressful white noise. I am ripe with jealousy that I can not enjoy the things so many people enjoy, and that I try so hard to enjoy them and fail.
